Terms and Conditions

  1. Tournament Discord Server: Each player must join the tournament Discord server. Failure to do so will result in disqualification.
  2. Face Camera Requirement: Players must have access to a face camera. Face cameras must remain on during the game.
  3. Screen Sharing: One of the players is required to share their screen in Discord during gameplay.
  4. Connection Failures: Connection failures caused by call interruptions, messages, notifications, etc., will not be considered.
  5. Punctuality: The tournament schedule is fixed. Players must be available at the specified time; otherwise, their opponent will be declared the winner of that match.
  6. Unavailability: If both players are unavailable during the scheduled match, they will each receive zero points.
  7. Registration and Payment: Players will only be registered for the tournament after completing the payment process.
  8. Match Duration:
    • All matches will be 10 minutes long.
    • Semi-final matches will be 12 minutes long.
    • The final match will be 15 minutes long.
